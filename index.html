What could you do with 350 hours of free consulting?

We are Team QMARK — a group of professional students working together to graduate Queen's Executive MBA. As part of our program, we are tasked with finding an organization outside of North America where we can provide free management consulting utilizing the course concepts that we have learned. Could you benefit from our services?

Free Management Consulting

We are seeking proposals from interested organizations with a business problem to be solved or an opportunity to be analyzed. The challenge may span a variety of areas, including finance, operations, technology, human resources, strategic planning, and marketing, or any area to which techniques and methodologies learned in our MBA program can be applied. Upon completion of the project, our team will produce a report of our work and findings and recommend a course of action for consideration by the organization's senior managers. Our team will fly to your location for a 5-10 day on-site consulting engagement.

About Queen's University

Queen's University — Smith School of Business is one of the world's premier business schools, with a rich academic tradition that dates back to 1841. The university is consistently ranked among Canada's top universities. The School has earned international recognition for its innovative approaches to business education and has been a pioneer in the areas of team-based and experiential learning.
